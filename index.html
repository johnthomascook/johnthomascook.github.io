Building businesses that are centered around the user and leverage data and technology to execute this vision is my passion. Following this passion is how I will push forward the human experiment.

I always have been a entrepreneur. Spent 10 years as a derivatives trader. Trading taught me that I enjoyed understanding human behavior and working with data. However, something was missing so I tried the corporate path. Worked four years as an Energy Marketer. Energy was great but...

My passion is technology and I wanted to dig deeper into...Digital Marketing, Analytics, Technology Interaction, Social psychology, User-centered design, Customer Development, and the Lean Startup. Grad school was the ideal place to engage these varied interests, and I enrolled at Northwestern University to study Integrated Marketing Communications.

Today my focus is taking this diverse set of knowledge and applying it to build startups & digital businesses.
